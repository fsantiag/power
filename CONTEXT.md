# Voltra — Domain Context

## Terms

**Voltra**
The product and brand. An EV charging point offered as a turnkey service — Voltra installs, operates, and bills for energy consumed.

**Ponto de Recarga**
The physical EV charging hardware unit installed at a location. Not sold outright; operated by Voltra.

**Condomínio**
The physical location where a Ponto de Recarga is installed. Sole target: condomínios residenciais (residential condos) in Natal, RN. Decision-maker is the síndico or administradora. Parking lots and other venues are out of scope for this phase.

**Síndico**
The condo manager — the primary decision-maker for Voltra installation. The main target persona for the landing page.

**Lead**
A síndico or condo administrator who submits the contact form expressing interest in Voltra installation.

**Modelo de Negócio**
Turnkey: Voltra covers installation cost. Revenue via energy billing per use — the end user (EV owner/morador) pays only for energy consumed. No upfront cost for the condomínio.
